Initial release of Lunachron (formerly Moonstone Companion).

• Full character compendium for all four factions
• Troupe builder with QR code import/export
• Active game screen with health, energy, and moonstone tracking
• Compact grid and detailed list layout modes
• Local multiplayer over Wi-Fi (NSD + TCP, no Google Play Services required)
• Solo play mode
• Campaign management and tracking
• Local tournament bracket support
• Optional character portrait downloads
• Automatic game data updates with user prompt
