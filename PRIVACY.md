# Privacy Policy

**LunaChron** (`io.github.garemat.lunachron`)

_Last updated: 2026-03-21_

## Summary

LunaChron does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Permissions

### Camera (`CAMERA`)
Used solely to scan QR codes for sharing troupes and compendium items between devices. No images are captured, stored, or transmitted.

### Location / Nearby Wi-Fi (`ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION`, `NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES`)
Used solely for local peer-to-peer Wi-Fi discovery (mDNS / Wi-Fi Direct) to connect devices for multiplayer games and tournaments. No location data is collected, recorded, or transmitted.

## Data Storage

All user data (troupes, campaigns, game results) is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. This data never leaves your device except when you explicitly share it (e.g. via a troupe share code).

## Network Access

The app may check GitHub (`github.com`) for game data and app updates. These requests contain no user data — only standard HTTP headers sent by your device.

No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs are included.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue at `https://github.com/garemat/moonstone-companion`.
